1. APP CONCEPT

What is NailCheck AI?

NailCheck AI is a photo-based AI diagnostic app that analyzes fingernail and toenail photos to identify potential health issues including: - Fungal infections (onychomycosis) - Nutritional deficiencies (zinc, iron, biotin, B12) - Circulation problems - Thyroid indicators - Early warning signs requiring medical attention
