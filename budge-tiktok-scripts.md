## Content Strategy Notes

### Posting Schedule:
- **3-5 videos per week** (consistency > virality)
- **Best times:** 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-10 PM EST (when ADHD folks are procrastinating or doom scrolling)

### Hashtags (mix 3-5 per video):
- **Core:** #ADHD #ADHDTikTok #ADHDProblems #ExecutiveDysfunction
- **Niche:** #ADHDHacks #ADHDSupport #ADHDCommunity #Neurodivergent
- **Broad:** #MentalHealth #Productivity #SelfCare #LifeHacks

### Hook Formula:
1. **Relatable pain point** (3 seconds max)
2. **Show the struggle** (visual + text overlay)
3. **Introduce Budge as solution** (demo app)
4. **Quick win/result** (before/after)
5. **CTA** (link in bio, try Budge)

### Visual Style:
- **Raw/authentic** (not polished influencer vibes)
- **Screen recordings** of app in action
- **Text overlays** for accessibility
- **Before/after** scenarios
- **Face-to-camera relatability** (tired, messy, real)

### Voice:
- **Conversational** (like talking to a friend)
- **Validating** ("This is real, you're not lazy")
- **No toxic positivity** ("Just do it" ‚ùå)
- **Empowering** ("Small steps count")

### Comments Strategy:
- **Engage with EVERY comment first 2 hours**
- **Ask follow-up questions** to boost engagement
- **Share more tips in replies**
- **Pin best testimonials**

### Duets/Stitches Opportunities:
- React to viral ADHD struggle videos
- Stitch "productivity hack" videos with ADHD-friendly version
- Duet with ADHD creators (cross-promo)
