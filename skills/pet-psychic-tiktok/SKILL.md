## Sourcing Pet Photos (Curated Approach)

### Where to Find Pets

**1. Pet Influencers (Best for reach)**
- Target: 10K-100K followers
- DM template: "Hi [name]! We love [pet name]. Would you let us feature them in a Pet Psychic reading? We'll tag you and promote your account to our followers."
- Rights: They give permission, you get content + their audience

**2. Stock Photo Sites (Unlimited supply)**
- Sources: Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay
- Search: "dog portrait," "cat funny face," "pet expressions"
- Filter: Commercial use allowed, high quality, expressive faces
- Target breeds: Golden retrievers (relatable), huskies (dramatic), tuxedo cats (judgmental), rescue mixes (emotional)

**3. Reddit (With permission)**
- Subreddits: r/aww, r/pets, r/animalsbeingderps, r/WhatsWrongWithYourDog
- Comment: "This pet has such personality! Would you mind if we featured them in our app? Full credit given."
- Most say yes for the exposure

**4. Personal Network**
- Friends, family, your own pets
- Easiest rights
- Personal stories = higher engagement

### Photo Selection Criteria

**Must-haves:**
- High quality (phone camera or better)
- Expressive face (judgmental, soulful, goofy, mysterious)
- Clear subject (pet fills 30%+ of frame)
- Natural or good lighting

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Context that adds story (messy room = chaos energy)
- Action shot (mid-bark, mid-jump)
- Eye contact with camera

**Avoid:**
- Blurry or low-res
- Multiple pets (confuses the hook)
- Stock-looking posed photos
- Sad/abused pet vibes

## Reading Style Guide

### Voice/Tone
- **Sassy best friend** â€” roast the owner lovingly
- **Dramatic main character** â€” pet thinks they're the protagonist
- **Wise old soul** â€” profound insights (for emotional angles)
- **Chaotic gremlin** â€” unhinged energy (for funny angles)

### What Works
- Specific complaints ("You sing off-key in the shower")
- Mild roasting ("Your cooking is... acceptable")
- Main character energy ("This is MY house, you're just the staff")
- Relatable pet behaviors ("I bark at the door but I'm scared of the mailman")

### What Doesn't Work
- Generic compliments ("Your dog loves you" â€” boring)
- Too mean (actually offensive, not funny)
- Too long (lose attention)
- Confusing (unclear who is speaking)

## Content Production Workflow

### Daily Workflow (AI Agent + Human)

**AI Agent Does (Automated):**
1. Select pet photo from curated library
2. Analyze expression/energy for hook angle
3. Write hook using proven formula
4. Generate reading using Pet Psychic app
5. Create 6-slide carousel:
   - Slide 1: Hook text overlay on pet photo
   - Slide 2: Screen recording of upload (5-sec clip)
   - Slide 3: Loading screen screenshot
   - Slide 4: Result screen
   - Slide 5: Reaction/personality photo
   - Slide 6: CTA with app screenshot
6. Write caption (hook + 3-5 hashtags)
7. Upload to TikTok as SELF_ONLY draft via Postiz API
8. Send WhatsApp message with:
   - Draft link
   - Caption text
   - Suggested sound type
   - Pet source (for credits)

**Human Does (60 seconds):**
1. Open TikTok draft
2. Pick trending sound that matches reading emotion
3. Paste caption
4. Add any on-screen text if needed
5. Hit publish
6. Reply to AI with "Posted" confirmation

## Failures to Avoid (Larry's Lessons)

**Learned from Oliver Henry & Larry:**

1. **Wrong image size** â€” Always 1024x1536 portrait
2. **Text too small** â€” 6.5% font minimum
3. **Text too high** â€” Bottom 15% only (avoid TikTok UI)
4. **Vague hooks** â€” Specificity wins ("my boyfriend" > "someone")
5. **No conflict** â€” Every hook needs tension/doubt
6. **Wrong sound** â€” Sound emotion must match reading emotion
7. **Posting at wrong time** â€” Test mornings vs evenings
8. **Giving up too early** â€” First 10 posts will suck. Keep iterating.
